FAQ

Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, Pocket Casts, and direct RSS. Use the buttons at the top of the page.

Will there be transcripts?

Yes—each episode page will include a transcript for accessibility.

How can I support the show without buying merch?

Share the podcast, leave reviews, and consider a one‑time tip or monthly pledge. It makes a big difference.
